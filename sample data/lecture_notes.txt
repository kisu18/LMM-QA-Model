Lecture 1: Introduction to LLMs
- Large Language Models (LLMs) are advanced NLP models.
- They are trained on diverse datasets.

Lecture 2: Transformer Architecture
- The Transformer model was introduced by Vaswani et al. in 2017.
- It uses self-attention mechanisms.

Lecture 3: BERT and GPT
- BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers) was introduced by Devlin et al. in 2018.
- GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) was introduced by OpenAI.
